About Us

We make good and
tasty food

Raja Sweet Shop is a full-service, fast-food-style Indian restaurant that has been instrumental in transforming this stretch of Hillcroft into what it is today: the Mahatma Gandhi District, home to many of the city’s Indian and Pakistani businesses. Raja Sweets was the first Indian Restaurant in Houston, and for over 31 years, we’ve been cooking up the best Indian sweets and fast food available! Stop by today!
